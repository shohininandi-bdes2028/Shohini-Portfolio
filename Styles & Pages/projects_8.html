Zine for Indian homemade remedies

Through this zine I want to remind the new generation that the homemade remedies are a part of our Indian culture and can be considered as health ka Jugaad as you don't need to go to a doctor and can get better at home

The style is inspired by Jamini Roy, a bengali art style as I am a bengali and wanted to add an element of my culture. The patterns and the colour schemes makes it engaging, defining my colourful nature


Fonts used are:

Headings-Copper black

Body-Sitka

Ingridients-Cameliya
