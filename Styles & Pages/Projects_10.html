Training App for the caregivers

Many family members and caregivers are required to care for people with dementia without proper training, guidance, or emotional support. This lack of knowledge makes caregiving stressful, confusing, and overwhelming, often affecting both the caregiver's well-being and the quality of care provided to the patient. There is a need for an accessible solution that helps caregivers learn, adapt, and feel supported while managing long-term dementia care.


The name of the app is Carely. Its an ongoing project.
