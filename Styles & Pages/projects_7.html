Typographic Poster & tickets for Swara Zankar music festival

The Swarazankar Music Festival is one of the most reputed festivals in India with a dynamic presence in more than 30 cities across the country since 2009. The typography concept is making instruments using words. The gaane ke bol, the tabla ke bol and the name of the artist form a tabla.

Why Tabla?

Tabla is an important part of the hindustani classical music. For any kind of instrument like sarangi, sitar, sarod, etc. or vocals, tabla is a requirement for accompanying the artist.
